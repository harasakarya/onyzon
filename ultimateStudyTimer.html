a problem.

College, high school, and middle school (maybe even elementary school) students struggle with finding the focus to study. They often can't seem to stay focused on their homework for more than 20 minutes without being distracted by the most mundane things.

feeding the issue.

Parents and teachers aren't helping. They pressure students to succeed and work hard, without understanding that the current system isn't functional. Students' minds just don't seem to function optimally during 4, 5, even 6 hour study marathons. The information isn't retained and the students lose the bit of motivation they had.

pomodoro.

The pomodoro technique is a method for maximum efficiency studying (or any type of work. We don't discriminate). By taking frequent, short breaks, and one longer break every so often, a student can maintain productivity for as long as he needs to, without feeling exausted, while also getting enough time to do enjoyable things over the course of the day.

Ultimate Study Timer.

Ultimate Study Timer is a sleek, dark themed timer that automatically times study periods and rest periods for you. All you have to do is pick your favorite study and break times, or choose your favorites, and the timer will alert you when it's time to study and when it's time to take a break.

instructions.

The Ultimate Study Timer is automatically set to 20 minute study sessions with five minute breaks. Every third break is a 25 minute break. Just press the start button to keep this default, or change the number fields and press set pattern to make this the new default. The timer will reset to the original default if you exit out and open it up again.
  Ultimate Study Timer has three unique vibration patterns to alert you when it's time to study, time to take a break, or if your timer session expires for some reason. Just keep your phone on the table or in your pocked and switch activities whenever prompted.

download.

The first incarnation of Ultimate Study Timer was a stand-alone desktop version that has been discontinued. If you have an android device, you can now download the android app completely free!
